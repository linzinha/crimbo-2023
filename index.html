FAQ

How can I play?

The easiest, best method is this form below. If it's not working or you're really not comfortable, you can kmail me but this is the best way to make sure you're included.

I got an error submitting my player name

If you get an error, doublecheck that your name isn't already on the list. If it's not, kmail me!

Where's my name?

It could be a number of reasons, actually.

  • This data is updated manually to make the page load more quickly: If you want to see realtime data to verify that your name was added, you can check the storage endpoint here, but note that it can take a good 8 seconds to load
  • The content is cached in your browser: try an incognito window
  • This is no longer true but in case it could be true again I'm leaving the reference here You entered your name beginning with a lowercase letter: Check after all the uppercase names

How does it work?

Each day I will randomize the winner of the day, and then the prize for the next day in /games. The timing has varied wildly and will continue to do so because I lack consistency, BUT it is between 7 and 9pm UTC most of the time.

When are you drawing until?

January 2nd because its my birthday!

Oh no my prize sucks!

It may secretly be amazing! The item drawn may be a REPRESENTATION of what you've won, but the actual prize will always be of equal or greater value than the item drawn.

Do I have to reenter every day?

Nope! If I do a call for entries, its just to get new/more people to join in the fun

Can I contribute?

If you like! However contributions are neither expected nor required. You can either send me items to add to my Display Case (the official prize vault), OR if you prefer to do so for high value items you can promise to deliver the item to the winner yourself. In that case I will list your ingame name next to the item to ID you as the owner of the prize.

How can I contribute?

Send me a kmail with your contribution or the name of the item you want me to list if you're holding onto it

  • Please tell me exactly what you want me to do with any items sent! For example:
  • Do you want me to list you anonymously?
  • Are you donating an item to be auctioned?
  • Is it a financial contribution to improve the prize pool?
  • Is it something else?
  • If the item isn't drawn, do you want it back?

What are you going to do with undrawn items

Right now, unless someone explicitly tells me to keep something I am going to either keep drawing until the pot is empty, give donations back, or both. Right now I am strongly leaning toward a lightning game of ARs in /games on the final day until everything is won.
