Advent Giveaway

Note: this site is extremely WIP while I focus on content for the calendar giveaway

To go along with Crimbo, I have decided to do a very simple giveaway. One prize per person per day. The prize and person are both pulled from their respective queues.

FAQ

How can I play?

The easiest, best method is this form below. If it's not working or you're really not comfortable, you can kmail me but this is the best way to make sure you're included.

I got an error submitting my player name

If you get an error, doublecheck that your name isn't already on the list. If it's not, kmail me!

Where's my name?

To make the page load more quickly, the list here is saved locally and cached. If you want to verify that your name was added, you can check the storage endpoint here,

How does it work?

Each day I will randomize an item and a player and give a gift to that player. NOTE: the item drawn may be a REPRESENTATION of the prize, but it will always be of greater value in that case. For example if you land on an IOTM I won't give you a consumable from said item. But if you land on a consumable it may be part of a pack of items.

Can I contribute?

If you like! However contributions are neither expected nor required. You can either send me items to add to my Display Case (the official prize vault), OR if you prefer to do so for high value items you can promuise to deliver the item to the winner. In that case I will list your ingame name next to the item to ID you as the owner of the prize.

Currently the prizes are based on what I can afford so if anyone wants to donate they can (no pressure) and the prizes will get better. If people choose to donate I will start a page with a list of donors and ow their donations were used.

Is there a way to get extra entries?

Maybe. I am considering a few possibilities, but none of them involve being able to buy more entries for personal gain. I don't want it to be weighted toward already rich players.
